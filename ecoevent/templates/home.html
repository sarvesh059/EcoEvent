Project Motive

Earth and the resources of earth make life possible on it. If we were to imagine our lives without these resources, that would not be possible. As life cannot function without sunshine, air, vegetation, and water. We can play our part in saving the environment by doing small tasks which may seem small but when done together can create bigger impact. The motive of this project is to provide a platform to come together and work together in harmony to multiply our efforts to save environment and leaving a better planet for future generation.

Project Description

The project is a platform that let's users to create events like Cleanliness drive, Afforestation drives, Awareness drives etc. and let's other users know about it so they can also take part in them. This project is created under Future Ready Talent Virtual Internship organised by Microsoft.
